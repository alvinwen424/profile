AtomizeThis

Web Scraping & Data Visualization

AtomizeThis is a web application that utilizes web scraping to create a visual map of the Wikipedia articles most relevant to a user-inputted topic.

How it works: Type anything you'd like into the search bar and we'll show you related Wikipedia articles as suggestions. Select one, press enter, and you'll see a visualization of your query surrounded by nodes representing the articles most relevant to it. Click any surrounding node and the process will repeat. Keeping clicking to go further "down the wiki hole."

A fun game: Enter two queries separately and try to connect them by clicking their branch nodes.

MediaGrabber

Clean & Minimalistic Design

Media Grabber fetches and parses image information from JSON data hosted at 'unsplash.it/list', then formats the images to custom fit your screen and makes them available for download.

Inspired by 'Splash Walls' by Nash Vail, I built this React-Native application in 36 hours for Fullstack Academy's 'Stackathon.' Credit for the idea goes entirely to Nash, the implementation is my own.

Places for Improvement: Optimization. Pictures occasionally take up to 30 seconds to render.
